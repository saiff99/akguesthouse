
# AK GUEST HOUSE — Complete Website

## Overview
A professional, welcoming single-page website for AK Guest House, targeting medical students, doctors, and visiting families. Clean, modern design with a calming color palette (deep navy, warm gold accents, soft whites) conveying trust, comfort, and professionalism.

## Sections & Layout

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Sticky header with logo "AK GUEST HOUSE", nav links to each section, and a prominent "Book Your Stay" CTA button
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width background image placeholder (exterior of guest house or campus view)
- **Headline:** "Your Home Away From Home — Steps From [Medical College Name]"
- **Subheadline:** "Premium accommodation designed for medical students, doctors, and families. Comfort, security, and focus — all under one roof."
- Two CTA buttons: "Book Your Stay" and "Inquire About Hostel"
- Trust badges row: 24/7 Security · Free Wi-Fi · Under 200m to Campus

### 3. Our Accommodations
- Two-column card layout with image placeholders
- **Basic AC Rooms** — affordable comfort with essential amenities (AC, Wi-Fi, attached bathroom, study desk)
- **Premium AC Rooms** — spacious, upgraded furnishings for visiting doctors/relatives (larger room, premium bedding, mini-fridge, seating area)
- Each card has amenity icons, starting price placeholder, and "View Details" button

### 4. Exclusive Girls' Hostel
- Highlighted section with distinct styling (soft accent background)
- Emphasis on safety (separate entry, female staff, CCTV), privacy, community living
- Amenities specific to hostel: common room, study area, mess facility access
- CTA: "Inquire About Hostel"

### 5. Rooftop Dining Restaurant
- Image placeholder of open-air rooftop setting
- Copy about hygienic, home-style meals, spacious seating, city views
- Mention of meal plans for long-term residents

### 6. Amenities & Facilities
- Icon grid layout (3×2 or responsive)
- 24-hour room service, high-speed Wi-Fi, 24/7 security & CCTV, free parking (bikes & cars)

### 7. Added Value Features
- Three feature cards with icons:
  - **24/7 Power Backup** — uninterrupted study and rest, peace of mind during exams
  - **Laundry & Housekeeping** — convenient services for busy schedules
  - **Quiet Zones / Study Lounges** — dedicated peaceful spaces for focused study

### 8. Testimonials
- Simple carousel/cards with placeholder quotes from students and parents

### 9. Contact & Location
- Two-column layout: inquiry form (name, email, phone, message, room type dropdown) + location info
- Address with "Under 200m walking distance" badge
- Phone, email, WhatsApp placeholders
- Embedded map placeholder
- Operating hours

### 10. Footer
- Quick links, contact info, social media icon placeholders, copyright

## Design Approach
- **Colors:** Navy primary (#1e3a5f), warm gold accent (#d4a853), clean whites and light grays
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif (Inter for body, bold headings)
- **Animations:** Subtle scroll-triggered fade-ins for sections
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first, all sections stack cleanly on small screens
- **Image placeholders:** Descriptive alt text indicating what photo to use (e.g., "Photo: well-lit AC room with study desk")
